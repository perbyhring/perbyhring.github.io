My name is Per Byhring. This is a page where I publish some things I make.

  • Slugs // Ημίθεος γυμνοσάλιαγκας
    This scene is inspired by a poem about slugs (but not really). The three.js scene is made up of one PlaneGeometry and one CylinderGeometry – no lights or out of the box materials. Everything is modeled and painted with GLSL vertex and fragment shaders.
  • Instanced Mesh Tunnel
    A tiny interactive experiments where I wanted to try out how to write InstancedMesh-instances declaratively with my svelte/three-library.
